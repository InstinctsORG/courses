Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Duration of the Sep 2024 Python Course?

The September 2024 course spans a total of 45 hours over 8 weeks.

Each week will include 6 hours of instruction, spread across 3 alternate days, with 2-hour sessions per day.

Will you provide Python Course Completion Certificate?

We provide the Python Course Completion Certificate in softcopy format. However, we strongly encourage candidates not to stop once they receive the certificate. Continue honing your skills and keep learning to stay ahead. Remember, no company will hire you solely based on a certificate—it's your skills and continuous growth that will set you apart.

Cheers & Happy Coding!!!

What if I miss half of the classes? Will I still be eligible for a refund?

Refunds are not applicable if you miss a significant portion of the course. We encourage to do active participation to ensure you gain the full value from the course.

Can I pay the course fee in installments or in the middle of the course?

Unfortunately, full payment is required before the course begins to secure your enrollment. This ensures a smooth experience for all participants and allows us to provide the best possible support throughout the course.

What if I miss a class? Can I get a recording of the missed session?

We do not provide recordings of missed classes. Initially, we offered this option, but it led to reduced engagement in live sessions, and many candidates eventually stopped attending. To maintain the interactive and effective nature of our live sessions, we’ve decided to focus on live participation.
